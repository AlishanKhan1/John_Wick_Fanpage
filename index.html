Boogeyman is the best place to play every game in the World of Assassination trilogy. All locations from Boogeyman 1 and Boogeyman 2 can be imported and played within Boogeyman 3 at no additional cost for existing owners – plus progression from Boogeyman 2 is directly carried over into Boogeyman 3 at launch.
